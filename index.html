Serving communities with compassion & dignity

Manav Sewa Kosh Trust works to improve health, education and livelihoods for the most vulnerable. We provide medical support, vocational training, scholarships, disaster relief and community development programs to empower lives and promote peace.

Education & Scholarships

Providing scholarships and learning opportunities in medicine, arts, science, engineering and technology.

Vocational training and self-employment initiatives for unemployed and handicapped individuals.

Relief & Humanitarian Aid

Rapid response and sustained relief during natural calamities and emergencies.

About Us

Mission

To advance human welfare and dignity by promoting health, education, and social development for all — particularly the marginalized and economically disadvantaged. We strive to empower individuals through access to healthcare, skills training, and educational opportunities, while fostering peace, compassion, and unity across communities and cultures.

Vision

We envision a compassionate and equitable world where every individual — regardless of background, gender, faith, or circumstance — has the opportunity to thrive.

Core Values

  • Compassion and Humanity
  • Inclusiveness and Equity
  • Integrity and Accountability
  • Empowerment and Self-Reliance
  • Collaboration and Partnership
  • Peace and Harmony
  • Service and Stewardship

Founder’s Message

At Manav Sewa Kosh Trust we believe every life has dignity and purpose. Our work is rooted in compassion and driven by a commitment to uplift those who face the greatest challenges. We invite you to join us in this mission — together we can create lasting change.
